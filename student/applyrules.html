PROCESS TO APPLY

  1. Go to APPLY UNIVERSITY
  2. THEN CHOOSE WHICH UNIVERSITY, YOU WANT TO GIVE ADMISSION AND CLICK OVER UNIVERSITY NAME WITH UNIT.
  3. BEFORE FILLUP THE FORM, COMPLETE THE PAYMENT OF THAT UNIT BY PHONE SMS.
  4. WRITE (UNIVERSITY SHORT FORM) (UNIT) (HSC ROLL) SEND IT TO 16328
  5. AN EXAMPLE, DU KHA 101035 SEND IT TO 16328.
  6. THEN YOU GET A CONFERMATION CODE AND WRITE IT TO REGISTER FORM TO COMPLETE YOUR UNIT ADMISSION.
  7. YOU CAN GET ADMIT CARD IN GMAIL.
